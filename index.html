About QR Codes

QR codes, short for Quick Response codes, are two-dimensional barcodes that store data. They can be scanned using a QR code reader or a smartphone camera equipped with the necessary software.

QR codes are widely used for various purposes, including product packaging, advertising, and mobile payments. They provide a quick and efficient way to access information with a simple scan.

Tips for QR Codes

  • Ensure there is enough contrast between the QR code and the background.
  • Test your QR code with different devices to ensure compatibility.
  • Avoid distorting the QR code – maintain its square shape.
  • Include a call-to-action near the QR code for better engagement.
